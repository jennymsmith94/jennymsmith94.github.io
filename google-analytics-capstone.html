Delivering Business Insights from a Rideshare Dataset

To complete the Google Data Analytics Certificate, I analyzed a large dataset of rides taken on bikes offered by a fictional rideshare company, "Cyclistic". The purpose was to extract insightful information about rider habits, specifically by comparing riders that were already subscription "members" vs "casual riders." The following is a series of insights meant to be presented to stakeholders in order to guide a marketing campaign aimed at converting casual riders to subscribing members.
